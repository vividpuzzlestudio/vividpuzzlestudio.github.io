Privacy Policy for Killer Item Sudoku

Killer Item Sudoku displays advertisements using Google AdMob.

The game itself does not require an account and does not collect names, email addresses, location, contacts, camera, microphone, or user-created content.

AdMob may collect or use device information, advertising identifiers, app interaction data, diagnostics, and related data to provide, limit, measure, and improve ads. Google may process this data according to its own policies.

The browser version and website use Google Analytics to understand page views and basic game interactions such as game start, clear, and game over. Google Analytics may collect usage data, device/browser information, and related analytics data according to Google's policies.
